HUONG DAN SU DUNG:

1. Sua file config.json:
   - Mo bang Notepad
   - Thay SUPABASE_URL va SUPABASE_KEY bang thong tin cua ban

2. Test thu:
   - Double-click "PCMonitor.exe"
   - Thay [OK] la thanh cong

3. Cai tu dong chay khi bat may:
   - Double-click "INSTALL_AUTOSTART.bat"
   - Chon 1 (Task Scheduler)

XONG! Tu gio may se tu dong gui du lieu len cloud.
